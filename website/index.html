WHO WE ARE

SportyTV is a linear, 24/7, free-to-air channel
established as the home of premium football in
Nigeria and Ghana.SportyTV is available via
StarTimes DTT (channel 258), DTH (channel 190),
and the OTT StarTimes ON in Nigeria and via DTT
(channel 36), DTH (StarTimes channel 190), and MultiTV in Ghana.
